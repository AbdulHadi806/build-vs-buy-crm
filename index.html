Conclusion

Unless your CRM needs are extremely unique and justify a long-term investment with full internal control, buying Salesforce is far more cost-effective and faster in terms of ROI—especially in Year 1.

Building internally becomes viable only if:

You already have idle developer capacity
You need deep customization not possible in Salesforce
You want to avoid per-user licensing in the long run (break-even in 3–4 years)
